IAC22 Workshops

IAC22 offers a curated selection of workshops for professionals of all levels. Workshops will take place April 18th through April 20th in a virtual format. Pricing varies depending on the format – single session three-hour workshops cost $125. Multi-session workshops, spread over two or three days, cost $200 each.

Student discounts are offered for eligible participants – $50 for a single session workshop and $75 for multiple session workshops.
